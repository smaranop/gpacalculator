Help & Support

Welcome to the GPA Calculator help page. Here you will find instructions on how to use the calculator effectively, along with tips for troubleshooting common issues.

How to Use the GPA Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your GPA:

  • Enter your name in the provided field.
  • For each academic year, add courses by clicking the "Add Course" button.
  • Fill out the class name, percentage grade, class type, and credits for each course.
  • Once all courses have been added for a year, you can add another year by clicking the "Add Year" button.
  • After entering all your courses, click the "Calculate GPA" button to see your unweighted and weighted GPA.
  • If you wish to schedule an appointment with a counselor, the option will be available based on your GPA.
  • You can also generate a transcript by clicking the "Generate Transcript" button.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using the GPA Calculator, consider the following tips:

  • Ensure all fields are correctly filled out for each course, including class name, percentage grade, class type, and credits.
  • Check your internet connection if you are experiencing delays or failures in loading the page.
  • Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser if the calculator does not seem to respond.
