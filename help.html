Help & Support

Welcome to the GPA Calculator help page. Here you will find instructions on how to use the calculator effectively, along with tips for troubleshooting common issues.

How to Use the GPA Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your GPA:

  • Enter your name in the provided field.
  • For each academic year, add courses by clicking the "Add Course" button.
  • Fill out the class name, percentage grade, class type, and credits for each course.
  • Once all courses have been added for a year, you can add another year by clicking the "Add Year" button.
  • After entering all your courses, click the "Calculate GPA" button to see your unweighted and weighted GPA.
  • If you wish to schedule an appointment with a counselor, the option will be available based on your GPA.
  • You can also generate a transcript by clicking the "Generate Transcript" button.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using the GPA Calculator, consider the following tips:

  • Ensure all fields are correctly filled out for each course, including class name, percentage grade, class type, and credits.
  • Check your internet connection if you are experiencing delays or failures in loading the page.
  • Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser if the calculator does not seem to respond.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add courses from previous years?

You can add courses from previous academic years by clicking the "Add Year" button for each year you need to add. Then, fill in your courses for each year accordingly.

Can I calculate both semester and yearly GPAs?

Yes, the GPA Calculator allows you to enter course information for both semester and full-year courses. Ensure you select the correct credit value for each course to accurately calculate your GPA.

What should I do if I make a mistake entering my course information?

If you make an error, you can either edit the course details directly if the option is available or delete the course and re-enter the correct information.

How is my GPA calculated if I retake a course?

For the purpose of this calculator, if you retake a course, only the highest grade should be entered to most accurately reflect the calculation methods used by many educational institutions.

Is this calculator suitable for college GPA calculation?

While this calculator provides a general estimate of your GPA, college students should consult their academic advisor or their institution's official GPA calculation policies to ensure accuracy, as weighting and grading scales may vary.
